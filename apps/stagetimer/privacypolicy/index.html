Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how privacy is handled in Stage Timer, developed by Alessandro Persiano.

Data Collection

Stage Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or any other type of data.

The app runs entirely on your device. No information such as name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage data, or analytics is collected.

Third-Party Sharing

Since no data is collected, no information is shared with third parties.

Security

All settings and data used by the app remain on your device and are not sent to external servers.
